# Trivia-Game

Welcome to my Trivia-Game!

It is 10 questions are on world capitals.  


1. Press “Start” to begin.
2. Click on the button with the text you believe is the correct answer.
3. You have 15 seconds to answer the question or you the question is logged as incorrect.
4. After 10 questions you will be given your score.  Press any key to reset the game and try again.

Some more features of the game:
1. The questions are randomized so you won’t get the same order of questions.
2. The answers are also randomized in case you wrote the position of the answer on your hand.
3. You will receive a fact about the country in question after every made answer.
